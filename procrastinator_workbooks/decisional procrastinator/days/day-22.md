## Understanding Complex Decisions (2 minutes)

**Multi-Dimensional Decision Analysis:** A systematic approach for handling choices that involve multiple competing factors, competing priorities, and complex trade-offs. This involves identifying all relevant criteria, weighing their importance, and finding the best overall balance rather than optimizing for single factors. *(Decisional procrastinators often struggle with complex decisions because they want to optimize everything perfectly.)*

Complex decisions involve multiple variables, considerations, and outcomes that affect your life. The key is breaking complexity into manageable components while maintaining perspective on what matters most to you. *(The goal is satisficing across multiple dimensions, not perfecting each one.)*

**Your complex decision patterns:** *(Check patterns that apply to you)*
□ Getting overwhelmed by too many variables
□ Losing sight of priorities amid complexity
□ Trying to optimize all factors simultaneously
□ Avoiding decisions that affect multiple life areas
□ Analysis paralysis with interconnected factors
□ Difficulty weighing competing needs

**What makes this decision complex?** *(Understanding complexity sources helps you address them systematically)*
□ Multiple life areas with different needs
□ Many variables to consider
□ Interconnected consequences
□ Long-term vs. short-term trade-offs
□ Uncertainty about multiple factors
□ Competing values or priorities
