## The Time Estimation Problem (2 minutes)

Most passive procrastinators consistently underestimate how long tasks take, leading to:
- Overpacked schedules that feel overwhelming *(example: planning 8 hours of tasks for a 4-hour Saturday)*
- Constant feeling of being "behind" on goals *(example: always pushing important tasks to "tomorrow")*
- Rushing through tasks or avoiding them entirely *(example: skipping exercise because "it takes too long")*
- Difficulty making time for priorities *(example: never starting creative projects because they seem huge)*

**Today's goal:** Start calibrating your time awareness to reality.
