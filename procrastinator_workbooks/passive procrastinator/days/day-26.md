### Step 2: Strategic Resource Framework (4 minutes)

**Design effective personal optimization systems:**

**Resource Decision Matrix:**
- **High skill required + I enjoy = Invest time to master**
- **High skill required + I don't enjoy = Find tools or simplified approaches**
- **Low skill required + I enjoy = Keep doing myself efficiently**
- **Low skill required + I don't enjoy = Automate, eliminate, or simplify**

**Optimization Process:**
1. **Clear outcome definition:** "What exactly needs to be accomplished?"
2. **Resource assessment:** "What time, energy, and tools are currently required?"
3. **Efficiency analysis:** "What's the minimum viable approach to achieve this outcome?"
4. **Implementation plan:** "When and how will I optimize this process?"
5. **Success criteria:** "How will I know the optimization worked?"
