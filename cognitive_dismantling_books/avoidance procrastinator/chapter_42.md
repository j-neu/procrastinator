### EXERCISE: The Share

**Objective:** To solidify your new identity.

1.  Tell one person (a friend, partner, or colleague) that you have adopted a new way of working.
2.  Don't preach. Just say, "I've stopped putting things off, and I feel amazing."
3.  Their reaction will likely be curiosity. That is your chance to help them.
