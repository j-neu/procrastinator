# The Easy Way to Stop Procrastinating (Emotion-Regulation Edition) - Structure

**Target Word Count:** 15,000 - 20,000 words
**Tone:** Informal, authoritative, empathetic, direct. No references to Allen Carr.
**Core Philosophy:** The Emotion-Regulation Procrastinator isn't "lazy" or "fragile"; they are addicted to *emotional relief*. They believe the lie that "I need to be in the right mood to work." The book dismantles this belief, showing that mood repair is a trap, not a prerequisite for action.

## Table of Contents
